Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

FitCheck collects minimal data to provide our service:

  • Photos you choose to upload for AI analysis
  • Google account information (email and display name for authentication)
  • Basic app usage data to improve our service
  • Purchase history for managing your Fit Checks balance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Photos are processed instantly by our AI system (Google Cloud Vision and Gemini) for style analysis and are not permanently stored
  • Your Google account information is used for authentication and to manage your Fit Checks balance
  • Usage data helps us understand how to improve the app
  • Purchase history is maintained to ensure proper delivery of purchased Fit Checks

3. Data Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • Photos are processed securely and immediately discarded after analysis
  • We never store your photos permanently
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • User data is stored securely in Google Firebase
  • You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Google Sign-In for secure authentication
  • Google Cloud (Gemini API) for AI processing
  • Firebase for user data storage and authentication
  • RevenueCat for managing in-app purchases
  • App Store and Google Play Store for app distribution and payments

5. Free and Paid Services

Our service structure:

  • New users receive 3 free Fit Checks upon signing up
  • Additional Fit Checks can be purchased through the app
  • Purchase history and Fit Check balance are stored securely

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access your current Fit Checks balance and purchase history
  • Delete your account and all associated data instantly through the app
  • Request information about your stored data
  • Opt-out of optional data collection

7. Contact Us
